## What's Ready for Your Review

The full marketing site is built and live at the link above. Here's what you'll see:

### Pages & Sections

- **Hero** — "Phone calls, handled." headline with Penny branding and a clear call to action
- **Social proof ticker** — a scrolling banner with key stats (calls handled, availability, satisfaction)
- **Features** — six cards covering what Penny does: 24/7 answering, appointment booking, emails and SMS, detail capture, human handoff, and system integrations
- **Use cases** — real-world scenarios: storage operators, property managers, trades businesses, and professional services
- **How it works** — a simple three-step breakdown: connect your number, Penny starts answering, review results

- **Contact form** — name, phone, email, and message. This form already sends submissions to our system so we'll receive leads as soon as the site goes live

### Design & Feel

The site uses a warm, human-centric design with copper tones and clean typography. It's fully responsive, so it works well on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Animations are subtle and purposeful, nothing flashy, just polished transitions as content comes into view.
